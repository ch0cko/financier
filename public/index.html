FINANCIER




Welcome to FINANCIER!

Financier is a simple website designed to help you stick to your own financial goals! From budget/expenses tracking to goal setting, with FINANCIER you can easily track your spending habits and have a constant reminder that you should stick to your financial goals outside of necessities and/or emergencies.



How to use FINANCIER?

Using financier is simple, just think of it as a notes app! Except FINANCIER is designed to be more streamlined and more efficient, saving you the hassle of turning to docs or spreadsheets to manage your budget and expenses.


To use FINANCIER, you simply have to click on the button on the bottom right of the main page, creating a budget goal/expenses tracker as you deem fit. This will then be displayed on the main page of the website, allowing you to create as many as you need.
